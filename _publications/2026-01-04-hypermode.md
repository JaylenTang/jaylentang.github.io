HyperMODE: A Continuous-Depth Spectral-Spatial Modeling Framework with Mamba and Neural Ordinary Differential Equations for Hyperspectral Image Classification

Accepted by [_IEEE Journal of Selected Topics in Applied Earth Observations and Remote Sensing_](https://www.grss-ieee.org/publications/journal-of-selected-topics-in-applied-earth-observations-and-remote-sensing/) (JSTARS), 2026, Volume 19.

HyperMODE combines multi-scale spectral-spatial representation learning with Mamba-based spatial propagation and neural ODE feature evolution.

Recommended citation: Tang, Jialin; Lou, Yunduan; Guo, Yanhui; Bai, Yu. (2026). "HyperMODE: A Continuous-Depth Spectral-Spatial Modeling Framework with Mamba and Neural Ordinary Differential Equations for Hyperspectral Image Classification." _IEEE Journal of Selected Topics in Applied Earth Observations and Remote Sensing_, 19.
