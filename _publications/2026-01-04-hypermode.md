HyperMODE: A Continuous-Depth Spectral-Spatial Modeling Framework with Mamba and Neural Ordinary Differential Equations for Hyperspectral Image Classification

Published at [_IEEE Journal of Selected Topics in Applied Earth Observations and Remote Sensing_](https://www.grss-ieee.org/publications/journal-of-selected-topics-in-applied-earth-observations-and-remote-sensing/) (JSTARS), 2026, Volume 19. DOI: [10.1109/JSTARS.2026.3705708](https://doi.org/10.1109/JSTARS.2026.3705708).

HyperMODE combines multi-scale spectral-spatial representation learning with Mamba-based spatial propagation and neural ODE feature evolution.
